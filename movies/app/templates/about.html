About Us

MovieHub is a movie booking and review management platform created to simplify movie discovery and ticket reservation.

Users can browse available movies, view show timings, check seat availability, and share reviews based on their viewing experience.

The project demonstrates how an online movie booking system can improve user convenience while providing valuable feedback through ratings and reviews.

Our key features include:

  • User Registration & Authentication
  • Movie & Show Management
  • Movie Search & Filtering
  • Ticket Booking & Seat Selection
  • Payment & Transactions
  • Reviews & Ratings
  • Notifications & Alerts
  • Admin Dashboard & Reports
